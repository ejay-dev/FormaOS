### App Description

**Short Description** (80 characters max):
> Operational Compliance Operating System - Streamline your compliance workflows

**Full Description**:

FormaOS is a comprehensive operational compliance operating system designed for modern businesses to streamline their compliance workflows, risk management, and audit processes.

**Key Features:**
• **Visual Node System**: Intuitive node and wire interface for managing compliance workflows
• **Plan-Based Access**: Flexible subscription plans with feature activation
• **Admin Dashboard**: Comprehensive administrative controls and oversight
• **Risk Management**: Advanced risk assessment and heatmap visualization  
• **Audit Trail**: Complete audit logging and compliance tracking
• **Team Collaboration**: Role-based permissions and team management
• **Evidence Management**: Secure document and evidence storage
• **Mobile Optimized**: Full-featured mobile experience with native gestures

**Perfect For:**
- Compliance managers and officers
- Risk management teams
- Internal audit departments
- Business operations teams
- Regulatory affairs professionals

**Security & Privacy:**
FormaOS employs enterprise-grade security measures including encrypted data transmission, secure authentication, and compliance with privacy regulations.

Transform your compliance operations with FormaOS - where operational excellence meets intuitive design.

### Review Process

#### Expected Review Times
- **iOS**: 1-7 days (24-48 hours typical)
- **Android**: 1-3 days (same day possible)

#### Common Rejection Reasons
1. **Missing functionality**: Ensure app works without network on basic features
2. **Login required**: Provide test account or guest access
3. **Broken links**: Verify all links work correctly
4. **Privacy policy**: Ensure comprehensive privacy policy
5. **In-app purchases**: Clearly describe subscription features

### Post-Approval Tasks

1. **Monitor reviews**: Respond to user feedback
2. **Analytics setup**: Configure app analytics
3. **Update schedule**: Plan regular updates
4. **Marketing assets**: Prepare promotional materials
5. **Support documentation**: Create user guides
