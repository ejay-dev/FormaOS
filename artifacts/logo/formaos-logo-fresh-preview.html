Opinionated round

Fresh directions I’d seriously consider for FormaOS.

This set is less about “showing the letters clearly” and more about building an ownable enterprise mark. I leaned into themes that fit the product: governed systems, signal clarity, defensible evidence, and operational continuity.

Option D
My safest pick

Lattice Gate

A structured, grid-aware mark that feels like policy, controls, and systems architecture. It reads as disciplined software rather than generic cybersecurity.

Systemic Product-native Distinct without being weird
Option E
Most ambitious

Signal Arch

Built around the idea of readiness and visibility: a platform that sends a clear signal outward. Warmer, more human, and more premium than the current brand.

Premium Not standard SaaS blue Strong marketing presence
Option F
Best metaphor fit

Evidence Prism

An abstract mark for layered evidence, auditability, and clarity. This one feels especially aligned with the “prove” side of the product story.

Audit-oriented Abstract brand asset Expandable system language
Option G
Most ownable silhouette

Axis Loop

A cleaner loop-based structure that suggests continuous operations and closed-loop compliance. Feels efficient, modern, and scalable across app surfaces.

Memorable outline Small-size friendly Modern enterprise
If I were choosing from this round alone, I’d push either Lattice Gate for a strong, credible platform identity or Evidence Prism if you want the brand to feel more unique and category-defining. Both feel closer to a company mark than just an app icon.
