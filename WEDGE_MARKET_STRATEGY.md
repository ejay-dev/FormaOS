# FormaOS Wedge Market Strategy

## Chosen Wedge

Australian regulated service providers, with the sharpest initial wedge in:

- NDIS providers
- disability and community care operators
- health-adjacent organizations with audit-heavy obligations

## Why This Wedge Fits The Current Product

- AU-first positioning already exists in trust and enterprise copy.
- The repo already contains strong healthcare / NDIS / care-oriented language and structures.
- Enterprise proof surfaces, trust packet, onboarding, and admin rescue workflows are valuable in these markets.
- Procurement and security review acceleration matter more in this segment than generic SMB productivity claims.

## What To Overfit For

- security review speed
- audit-ready exports
- operator rescue for activation and billing risk
- clear procurement answers
- industry-specific onboarding and value proof

## Messaging Direction

- “Compliance operating system for regulated Australian service providers”
- “Run security review, rollout, and audit preparation in one governed path”
- “Reduce parallel spreadsheets, fragmented evidence, and last-minute audit scramble”

## Product Moves That Support The Wedge

- stronger activation dashboards for regulated customers
- more buyer-proof surfaces tied to trust and procurement
- customer-success playbooks for first 14/30/60 days
- lifecycle and rescue controls that match enterprise support expectations

## What Not To Do Right Now

- broaden the story to every possible framework and industry at the same intensity
- lead with generic compliance software language
- over-invest in horizontal features before the wedge story is undeniable
